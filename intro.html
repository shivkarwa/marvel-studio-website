Upcoming Movies


  1. Ant-Man and the Wasp
    Ant-Man and the Wasp is an upcoming American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics characters Scott Lang / Ant-Man and Hope van Dyne / Wasp. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, it is intended to be the sequel to 2015's Ant-Man, and the twentieth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

  2. Captain Marvel
    Captain Marvel is an upcoming American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Carol Danvers / Captain Marvel. A film based on Carol Danvers has been in development at Marvel Studios since as early as May 2013. A release date was first set in October 2014, and Perlman and LeFauve were hired as a writing team the following April after submitting separate takes on the character. Larson's casting was announced at the 2016 San Diego Comic-Con International, making it Marvel Studios' first female-led solo film.

  3. Avengers 4
    Avengers 4, is an upcoming American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. It is intended to be the direct sequel to 2018's Avengers: Infinity War, as well as the sequel to 2012's Marvel's The Avengers and 2015's Avengers: Age of Ultron and the twenty-second film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

  4. Spider-Man: Homecoming sequel
    The film, which is a direct sequel to 2017's Spider-Man: Homecoming, is said to deal with the aftermath of The Avengers 4 and help the Marvel Cinematic Universe transition into its next era.
